At Mixd, we specialise in web accessibility and have been designing
accessible websites that meet WCAG standards for almost 20 years.
Designing and building accessible digital services is a key feature of
our delivery process, aligning fully to the Government Service
Standard. As well as offering accessibility training and testing within
projects, we also offer accessibility audits and consultancy to support
compliance with the Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations.
